CH-MAG-02 . family MAG

PAIRED COLUMN

two columns per category, side by side. Needs: ordered categories + exactly two comparable series (budget vs actual).

magnitudecomparison

Use when

  • two measures per category: budget vs actual, plan vs forecast
  • 6 categories or fewer

Do not use when

  • more than two series (unreadable; use lines or small multiples)
  • long category names (use CH-DST-02)

See instead

  • more than two series: CH-TIM-01

Alternatives

  • CH-DST-02 (dots when columns crowd)

Rules

  • series named by direct labels above the first pair, never a legend
  • series_a muted (reference), series_b primary (actual/key)
  • bars start at zero
  • unit once

Never do this

  • three or more series per group
  • legends
  • truncated baseline

QA before delivering

  • exactly two series
  • labels above first pair
  • baseline at zero
  • unit once
  • source present

Data shape

titleaction title naming the widest gap
sourcebottom-left always
uniton first value label
xordered category labels
seriesexactly two: [{name, values}, {name, values}]; second is the key
